Fine Arts (Hobby Course)

We offer you the opportunity and platform to hone your hobby and transform it into an art. We are dedicated to foster the skills of emerging talent and are driven to nurture artists that can create a more humane world. At our institute, we teach our students drawing and painting in a pragmatic way that helps them build their confidence and get more professional about their artistic abilities as well as skills. 

We offer courses in different fields of fine art and we follow a special approach in imparting the training regarding drawing, sketching and painting. Our course curriculum is quite comprehensive and we offer following 



Hobby Certification Course: (Regular)

We offer you the platform for honing your hobby and transforming it to ART.

Course Curriculum :

Drawing and Sketching

Drawing and sketch classes offered by us help teach students to create quickly sketches and drawings using tools like pencils and pens. We make students learn drawing methods and theory along with figure drawing and provide a perfect platform to them to become a better artist over time.

Colour concepts

Art is all about painting and it requires the application of vivid colours to bring life to a particular piece of art. We at Art Palette are dedicated to providing training on the application of colours in painting and helping our students become a better painter.

Still life

Still, life painting is the work of art that focuses on creating subject matter which is inanimate and where natural objects like food, flower and plants are created. This form is art is quite creative and we with the help of our expert professionals make sure that student learns this form of art in a perfect manner.

landscape

It is the creation of natural sceneries depicting mountains, valleys, trees, rivers and forests. A creative form of painting, we provide hands-on training for learning landscape designing in most creative ways using perfect shades and colour combinations.

Portraits

One of the most creative forms of painting, portrait making is concerned with representing the personality of a person through special features. Portrait experts with our institute are highly professional and impart training in an ideal manner to train students in portrait making.

Figurative Study

This course is quite vivid and includes model settings that span into several sessions. The course is extensively designed to make students learn substantial pieces of work as the course duration progresses with increased difficulty level. Figurative art study course offered by us is highly unique and develops the skills of students in this field quite extensively.

Abstract art

A different type of art making, the abstract is concerned with exploring forms and colours for depicting emotions in a subtle way. We at Art Palette are driven to provide students with best abstract art training and help them learn the technique while enhancing their overall capabilities and creative skills.

Painting

The ability to use colours and paints for illustrating an art or any decoration, painting classes provided by us help students learn the application of colours and paints in a unique and creative manner. We have at our disposal the services of professional painters who taught students about varied techniques of painting and help become professional painters.

Course Duration : Continuing

Classes : 2 days a week, 8 classes a month

Two Year Certification Course

Course Curriculum:
